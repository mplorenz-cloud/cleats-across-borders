Cleats Across Borders

Providing soccer cleats and athletic shoes to young athletes across Central America.

Our Mission

Growing up playing soccer in the United States, a new pair of cleats is routine. We grow up around resources that make playing organized sports easy, and oftentimes take them for granted.

For many around the world this isn't the case, and a new pair of cleats could be the difference between playing the game so many of us love versus sitting on the sidelines.

Cleats Across Borders is a student-led nonprofit dedicated to collecting and distributing soccer cleats and athletic shoes to under-resourced communities.

We believe that access to proper equipment empowers youth, builds confidence, and opens doors through sport.

How It Works

  1. We collect gently used or new cleats
  2. We clean and sort them by size
  3. We partner with local organizations to distribute them directly to athletes
